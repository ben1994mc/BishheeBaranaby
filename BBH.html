About

The Bishee Barnabee Hotel (BBH) is a newly established independent hotel located on the western periphery of Norwich. The BBH is situated within walking distance of the commercial hub of the city, the hospital and the world-leading research park are nearby, facilitating all business needs and requirements.

  • Stay fit after a decadent night at our newly developed gym facility
  • Secure successful business deals in our meeting rooms
  • Have an unforgettable private event in our function rooms
  • Enjoy world class food in our Michelin star restaurant

Nothing is off the table as far as we’re concerned. To cope with limited availability, we provide a dedicated online service to manage your bookings.

Function Rooms
