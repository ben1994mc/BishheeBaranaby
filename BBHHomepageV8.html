About Us

The Bishee Barnabee Hotel (BBH) is a newly established independent hotel located on the western periphery of Norwich. We are within walking distance of the commercial hub of the city and the ground-breaking Norwich Research Park. With a range of facilities and function rooms available, we are able to cater to your every need, be it business or leisure. Come and stay for as long as you like! Nothing is off the table!

Take a look around:

Reception Lobby Main Dining Room Main Function Room

We have been providing comfortable home-from-home stays and an extravagant and indulgent experiences for generations. Norwich's history is well defined, and so is ours: Our world class service dates back over 50 years, 1000+ weddings, and innumerable business functions!

We pride ourselves on our Michelin Star restaurant, state of the art facilities, friendly, hardworking staff and tailored service.

Check out the top local attractions in our fine city, such as Norwich Cathedral, Norwich Castle, and Norwich Research Park.

Norwich's booming nightlife is easily accessible from the Bishee Barnabee. With a range of late night public transport options and a late night taxi service run by the BBH itself, you're guaranteed to arrive on time, and get home safely from anywhere within 40 miles of your home away from home.
