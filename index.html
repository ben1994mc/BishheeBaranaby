About

The Bishee Barnabee Hotel (BBH) is a newly established independent hotel located on the western periphery of Norwich, within walking distance of the commercial hub of the city. The hospital and research park are nearby, facilitating all business needs and requirements. Accommodation comes in two basic forms: double or twin, supplemented with either a basic package or premium facilities. Alongside this we provide a range of services to meet your needs. Come dine with us at the fashionable Bishee Barnabee restaurant (BBR). Stay fit after a decadent night at our newly developed gym facility. Nothing is off the table as far as we’re concerned. We provide a dedicated online service to manage your bookings. Stay with us. You can stay as long as you like.

Room Information
Double Twin

Our Double (19 m² / 204 sqft) room is perfect for couples and guests who like extra amenities. Our double bed is developed with special memory foam uniquely designed for a therapeutic and comforting stay.

Our Twin-Sharers (23m²/2010 sqft) room is suited perfectly for the needs of two travellers. Each bed is equipped specially designed luxurious memory foam to make every night you stay with us is the best sleep you will ever have.
